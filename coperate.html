About Our School

Excellence in Elementary Education

At Sunrise Elementary, we believe every child has the potential to shine. Our dedicated teachers and supportive environment help students develop strong foundations in academics, creativity, and character.

Since 1995, we have been committed to providing quality education that prepares our students for success in middle school and beyond.

Join our school family and watch your child flourish in a nurturing, engaging learning environment.
